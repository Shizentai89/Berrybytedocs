**BerryByte** is a powerful server provider for all your server needs without breaking the bank! If you'd like to purchase a server or learn more, visit our site at https://berrybyte.net/

**Find yourself stuck?** We have a trained support team ready at any time of the day to answer your most simple and complicated questions. You can learn more about getting the correct type of support [here](/support)

**"How do I ... ?"** You've most likely ran into an issue before that only needs a quick fix, which is why we've created this documentation site that is super rich in pages to help you combat some simple errors you may encounter. If your question is still unanswered, you can [open a ticket](https://berrybyte.net/tickets).

These guides are designed for BerryByte users, however the same guides can be applied most certainly be applied in other environments.
